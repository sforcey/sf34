Poster ideas.





Sample outline:

  • Abstract: Just a few sentences introducing your topic.
  • Definitions (list the terms and ideas you plan to define.)
    • examples illustrating terminology (briefly describe the examples you plan to give including diagrams.)
  • Open problem(s) i.e. No solution known! (state the open problem briefly.)
    • examples illustrating the open problem(s)
  • What is known. (List some known estimates, partial solutions and bounds for unknown solutions.)
  • Optional experiment. (Describe what you plan to put in your poster as experimental math.)
  • Sources: At least three, including websites.
