• Splitohedra <em> Sp_n </em> [<a href = "https://arxiv.org/abs/1608.01622">arxiv</a>] (S. Forcey, L. Keefe, W. Sands)
<br>
• relaxation of the  <a href="#BME">Balanced Minimal Evolution Polytope</a> BME(n).<br>
• intersection of half-spaces{<em>split-facets, intersecting cherry faces, <br>caterpillar facets and the cherry clade-faces</em>} from BME(n)<br> and also obeying the {<em>Kraft equalities</em>}. <br>
<td>
