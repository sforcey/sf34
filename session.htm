Welcome!
    This session will hopefully bring together people working in some of the intersections above, both to catch up
    on what old friends are working on and to hear from new friends about the way some of our favorite mathematical
    objects are being used in diverse applications.
    We would love to hear talks about enumerative combinatorics, geometric combinatorics, species and Hopf algebras.
    We'd really like to see connections to category theory and deformation theory.
    Speakers are invited to talk about whatever they think is exciting!

    Here is the speaking schedule. Talks are linked to abstracts, which list additional authors.
    All talks are in Room 138 of the College of Arts and Sciences building. Map and photo of building... close parking... and more: lot 14 (Olin Hall parking B).
