    Ramsey numbers.


  • Ramsey theory [wiki]
  • What is the smallest complete graph K_n with edges colored (red or blue) such that there is either a red or a blue complete subgraph of size k?
  • n = R(k,k) is shown to exist.
  • Specific Question: What is R(5,5)? [wiki]
  • Best known answer: R(4,4) = 18, 43 <= R(5,5) <= 48.
  • Conjectured Sequence:
    0, 1, 2, 6, 18, 45, 102, 213, 426, 821, ... [ OEIS ]
